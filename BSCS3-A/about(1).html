Taylor Swift's Biography

Taylor Alison Swift (born December 13, 1989) is an American singer-songwriter, actress, and cat-lover...

She has sold over 50 million albums worldwide and won 10 Grammy Awards, an Emmy, and 6 Guinness World Records...

Taylor Swift's Timeline

  • December 13, 1989 - Taylor Swift was born in Reading, Pennsylvania
  • October 24, 2006 - Released debut album Taylor Swift
  • November 11, 2008 - Released sophomore album Fearless (which includes hits like "Love Story")
  • October 22, 2010 - Released album SPEAK NOW
  • October 24, 2012 - Released album Red (including iconic songs like "I Knew You Were Trouble")
  • October 27, 2014 - Released album 1989 - her first "official" pop album
  • November 10, 2017 - Released album Reputation
  • August 23, 2019 - Released album Lover
  • December 11, 2020 - Released surprise albums Folklore and Evermore
  • October 21, 2022 - Released album Midnights (which includes "Anti-Hero")
  • April 19, 2024 - Released album The Tortured Poets Department (anticipated new release)
